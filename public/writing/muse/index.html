muse ariadne

muse ariadne is a writing club that posts weekly prompts. i'm not sure how often i'll write here, but it might be fun to be a part of something like this.

archive

week of apr 01: explore the concept of time in your writing. play with the idea of how we perceive passing time [linear/cyclical/all at once/not at all] and make it weird and surreal, or maybe go more classic & write some fun time travel/time loop fiction. how does time shape us?

week of mar 25: try writing in the second person. address your audience and sit with them as you tell your story. see how this direct connection affects how you write.
